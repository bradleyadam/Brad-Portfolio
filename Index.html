About Me

I'm an indie game developer from the UK, comfortable working with a variety of game engines. By day, I work in retail, but my real passion comes alive after hours, where I focus on creating the kind of games I love to play.

My core design philosophy is simple: player-first. I believe games should be rewarding and enjoyable on their own merits. That's why I'm committed to avoiding predatory microtransactions and pay-to-win mechanics. Skill, strategy, and experience should be what drive success, not the size of your wallet.

When I'm not coding or at my day job, you'll probably find me cheering on my football team or getting lost in a good zombie survival game—two genres that heavily influence my creative process and project ideas.

Core Principles
